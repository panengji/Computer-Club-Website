USER ADMIN

Are you a student? Pick "User"

  • You are a CCS student who primarily interacts with the website to access information, resources, or participate in activities.
  • Your main role is to consume content, engage with the community, and utilize the features provided by the website.
  • You may have limited access to certain features or administrative functionalities, focusing more on utilizing the platform's services rather than managing them.
  • Your interactions are typically centered around personal use, learning, or participating in activities related to your interests or objectives.

Are you part of the organization? Pick "Admin"

  • You are typically an adviser, club leader, or someone responsible for managing and overseeing the operations of the website or a specific section of it.
  • You have additional responsibilities compared to regular users, including the ability to make decisions, implement changes, and maintain the website's integrity and security.
  • Your interactions involve monitoring, editing, and examining content, as well as providing administrative support, organizing events, and demonstrating leadership qualities.
  • Your role involves administrative tasks such as managing user accounts, creating and moderating content, enforcing rules and policies, and ensuring the smooth functioning of the platform.
